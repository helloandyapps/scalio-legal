1. Summary

Scalio stores all information about your collection exclusively on your local device. We don't operate our own server, don't collect or see your data, and don't use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

2. What data is processed

Collection data (names, brands, prices, photos, notes, statuses) is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData and never transmitted to external servers. Backups (JSON/CSV export) are created locally — you decide where to save them.

3. Device permissions

Camera (scanning QR codes), Photo Library (adding a model photo, via the private system picker), Notifications (local reminders).

4. Third-party services

The "eBay" button opens eBay's page in an in-app browser — eBay's policy applies there. Instagram/Telegram/Email links open the respective apps.

5. In-app purchases

Processed exclusively through the App Store (Apple StoreKit). We never see your card details.

6. Analytics and advertising

Not used.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13.

8. Data deletion

Deleting the app permanently deletes all local data (unless you've made a backup). A "Delete Collection" function is available in Settings.

9. Policy changes

We'll notify you of material changes through an app update.
